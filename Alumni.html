Rev.Fr.S.Sebastian M.A.B.Ed.

Headmaster

Hello Little Flowers

It gives me privilege and pleasure to address and greet you all from this noble Institution. Our School has marked historical foot prints on the sands of time as she is in her Nonagintennial (90th) year of existence. All through these years our alma matter had begotten illustrious sons of fame in various fields like arts, science, sports and services. All these sons are spreading her fragrance all over the world. Some are in touch with but many are not. She has grown gorgeous since then. In another decade as she is entering her centennial year I wish all her luminous sons to register themselves in the portal specially provided. Let us put all our mind and strength together to take our Little Flower School in to her glorious centennial birth.
